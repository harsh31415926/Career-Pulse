Career Acceleration
Happens Here

Our firm is a launchpad for those who embrace opportunity, own their advancement and realize their full potential. Here’s an example of accelerated learning and growth. It’s one of many.

Ben’s five-year journey to Portfolio Manager

Ben’s five-year journey to Portfolio Manager

Year 1:

Associate

Joined a seasoned team of exceptional stock pickers and mentors. Developed industry expertise, management relationships and built detailed financial models with the goal of driving idea generation for ~30 companies based on deep fundamental diligence.

Year 3:

Senior
Associate

Continued to hone modeling and idea generation skills, deepen industry relationships and gain experience. Learned about portfolio construction and sizing.

Year 4:

Analyst

Started taking risk, running my first portfolio while mentoring a new associate.

Year 5:

Portfolio
Manager

Managing and scaling a portfolio that includes video games, internet, media, software, cable and telecom. Leading and teaching a team of four.

“I am inspired and encouraged everyday by my peers who are some of the most talented minds in the industry. With their guidance, and unparalleled access to capital and infrastructure, I've learned quickly, honed my skills and swiftly taken on responsibility.”

Ben Global Equities Portfolio Manager
