Frequently asked questions

UBS offers a wide range of banking jobs, including investment banking jobs, wealth management jobs, trading jobs and roles in retail across global locations.

To apply for financial advisor jobs or financial analyst jobs, visit the UBS Search jobs page, search for open positions and submit your application online.

Credit analyst jobs require strong analytical and financial assessment skills, while trading jobs call for quick decision-making, market knowledge and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.

Yes, UBS regularly recruits for private equity jobs and investment banking jobs, offering opportunities to work on high-profile deals and strategic financial projects.

Wealth management jobs at UBS include positions such as relationship managers, portfolio advisors and investment specialists, all focused on helping clients achieve their financial goals.

Yes, UBS provides remote banking jobs and flexible work arrangements, including remote and hybrid options, to support work-life balance and employee wellbeing.

UBS is committed to supporting employees through change, offering mentoring and internal mobility opportunities to help you grow within the organization.

The recruitment process for banking jobs at UBS typically involves an online application, assessment tests, interviews and, for some roles, case studies or technical evaluations.
